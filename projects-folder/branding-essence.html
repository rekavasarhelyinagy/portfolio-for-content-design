Branding - Perfume

This is individual project is a perfume branding. The slogan seen on the poster below "Let the light into your life" refers to the enlightened emotions the brand aims to evoke in customers.
The idea behind placing the logo not on top of a sticker like most the perfumes, but engraving it on the transparent surface is to allow the light shine through the glass, creating magical colors and heart-warming, romantic, delightful emotions. This picture also appear on the product poster below, where the perfume is presented with a rising sun in the background, which gives the glass beautyful light colours.
