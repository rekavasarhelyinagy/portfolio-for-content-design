About me

I am Réka Vásárhelyi-Nagy from Hungary.
After completing my bachelor's degree in International Relations studies in English at ELTE university of Budapest, I decided to change the direction in my carreer and follow my passion for designing creative products.
I am currently in my second year of New Media Design at Jönköping University and looking for an internship opportunity to try myself out in practice and gain work-experience in the industry.

I study diverse areas of media design, such as graphic design, ux/ui design and web development.
Besides learning the technical background for these niches, I gained many more skills, which are useful for my future career, as for as team-work, project management, marketing strategies, business oriented and entrepreneurial mindset.

My goal now is to practice and widen my skillset within the field so I can have a wide insight and understanding of more areas and finally find my niche in the industry I am the best at and enjoy
most doing.

I can imagine myself in the future designing and developing websites using my communitaction and multicultural skills to understand the needs of the clients from any part of the world, and taking advantage of my UX/UI, front-end development and graphic design skillsets to create user friendly, visually appealing products.
